# My Hackathon Experience: Competing in REIMAGINE

Hackathons are intense, fast-paced events where developers, designers, and problem-solvers come together to create innovative solutions within a short time frame. I had the incredible opportunity to participate in the **REIMAGINE Hackathon by Sheryians Coding School**, where I competed in **four rounds** and secured **4th position**.

## The Challenge
The goal of the hackathon was to **redesign and enhance major brand websites** with a fresh, interactive, and engaging experience. I took on the challenge to **redesign the NIKE and Xbox websites**, focusing on modern UI/UX, performance, and smooth animations.

## The Rounds
### **Round 1: NIKE Website Redesign**
- Redesigned the **NIKE website** with a modern approach.
- Implemented smooth animations using **GSAP** for better user interaction.
- Focused on minimalism, responsiveness, and performance.

### **Round 2: Xbox Website with 3D Models**
- Created an interactive **Xbox website** with **3D models**.
- Used **Three.js** to add engaging visuals and animations.
- Improved user engagement with smooth transitions and an immersive experience.

### **Round 3: Interview Round**
- Faced a **technical interview** where I explained my **approach** to redesigning both websites.
- Discussed the thought process behind the UI/UX improvements.
- Shared insights on the challenges and solutions during development.

### **Final Round: Website Redesign Challenge**
- Received a **new website** to redesign within a limited time.
- Applied all previous learnings to create a sleek and user-friendly interface.
- Though I couldn't win, I secured **4th position**, making it an unforgettable experience.
